Warm-Up 18
  1. There are 240 pairs of numbers such as {7,5318} or {17,358} that can be formed using each of the digits 1, 3, 5, 7 and 8 exactly once. What is the largest possible product of two such numbers?
  2. If a rectangle with an area of 400ft2 is divided into two smaller rectangles with areas in the ratio of 3:1 , what is the least possible value of the perimeter of the smaller rectangle?
  3. A set of seven different positive integers has a mean of 13. What is the positive difference between the largest and smallest possible values of its median?
  4. When written in a certain positive base b, 363 (base 10) is 123 (base b). What is the value of b?
  5. Rhombus EFGH is inscribed in rhombus ABCD with point E on AB⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯ , point F on BC⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯ , point G on CD⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯ and point H on AD⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯ . If AE:EB=BF:FC=CG:GD=DH:HA=1:2 , and if the area of rhombus ABCD is 180units2 , what is the area of rhombus EFGH?
  6. The solutions x=u and x=v of the quadratic equation rx2+sx+t=0 are reciprocals of the solutions of the quadratic equation (2+a)x2+5x+(2−a)=0 for some integer a. If the GCF of r, s and t is 1, what is the value of r+s+t ?
  7. A club with 22 students is forming a committee of either 4 or 5 students. What is the ratio of the number of different 4-person committees possible to the number of different 5-person committees possible? Express your answer as a common fraction.
  8. Quadrilateral ABCD has vertices at points A(-9, 4), B(-7, 8), C(-3, 6) and D(-6, 2). Quadrilateral WXYZ is congruent to quadrilateral ABCD and has vertices W(2, -3), X(4, 1) and Y(8, -1) and a fourth vertex, Z. What is the sum of the coordinates of vertex Z?
  9. A right triangle has integer side lengths a, b and c with a<b<c . If a+c=49 , what is the area of the triangle?

  10. The cube with vertices A, B, C, D, E, F, G, H has edges of length 2 units. Point M is the midpoint of EH⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯ and N is the midpoint of EF⎯⎯⎯⎯⎯ . What is the volume of the tetrahedron with vertices A, C, M, N? Express your answer as a common fraction.
