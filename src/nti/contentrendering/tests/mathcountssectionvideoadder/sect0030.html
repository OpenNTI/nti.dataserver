Sequences Stretch
  1. What is the 100th term of the arithmetic sequence 3, 11, 19, 27, … ?
  2. What is the sum of the first 100 terms of the arithmetic sequence 3, 11, 19, 27, … ?
  3. What is the 10th term of the geometric sequence 729, 243, 81, 27, … ? Express your answer as a common fraction.
  4. The 1st and 18th terms of an arithmetic sequence are 4 and 8.25, respectively. What is the 35th term of the sequence? Express your answer as a decimal to the nearest tenth.
  5. The first three terms of an arithmetic sequence are p, 2p+6 and 5p−12 . What is the 4th term of this sequence?
  6. All terms in a geometric sequence are positive integers, and the first three terms are n, n+3 and 2n+6 . What is the 4th term of this sequence?
  7. The 3rd term of an arithmetic sequence is 17, and the 9th term is 83. What is the 1st term?
  8. The 2nd term of a geometric sequence is 24, and the 5th term is 81. What is the 1st term?
  9. The 6th term of an arithmetic sequence is 24. What is the sum of the 5th and 7th terms?
  10. The number of bacterial cells within a Petri dish doubles every hour. If there are 8 cells in the dish at the end of the 2nd hour, how many cells will be in the dish at the end of the 8th hour?
