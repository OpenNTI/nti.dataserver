Manage Your Account
ACCESSING THE ACCOUNT MENU

The “Account Menu” is located in the upper right-hand corner of the browser. By clicking on the gear icon, you can personalize your account and view information about NextThought.

  • Notifications appear when members of your community share an “Interaction” (e.g., Highlight, Note) with you or respond to an Interaction you shared with them. Clicking on the notification takes you to the specific Interaction in the content.

  • My Account opens your account preferences.

  • About links to our website.

  • Help gives you direct contact to us via email.

  • Privacy allows you to review our privacy policy.

  • Sign Out signs you out of your NextThought account and returns you to the log-in page.

ADDING A GRAVATAR AND ALIAS

Account preferences are accessible by clicking “My Account.”

  • Alias allows you to change the name that is viewable to your NextThought community.

  • Gravatar is your profile image. You can add or change your profile image by following the directions at gravatar.com.
