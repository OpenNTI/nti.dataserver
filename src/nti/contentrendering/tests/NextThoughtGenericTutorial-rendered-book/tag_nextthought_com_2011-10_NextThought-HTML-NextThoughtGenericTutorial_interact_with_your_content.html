Interact with Your Content
ADDING AND DELETING A HIGHLIGHT

A “Highlight” is a way to emphasize valuable information within your content.

  1. Click and drag the cursor over the desired text.

  2. Click “Save Highlight” in the menu that appears.

  3. To delete a Highlight, select the Highlight you want to remove. Then click “Delete Highlight” from the menu.

ADDING AND DELETING A NOTE

A “Note” is a personal annotation you can make to your class content. There are two ways to add a Note to the text.

Option 1

  1. Click and drag the cursor over the desired text.

  2. Click “Add Note” in the menu that appears.

  3. Type your comment into the box and click “Save.” The Note will appear in the margin to the left of the selected text, and the text will be highlighted.

  4. To delete the Note, hover your cursor over the Note. Click the drop-down icon and click “Delete.”

Option 2

  1. Hover your cursor over the left margin. An option to “Write a Note” will appear.

  2. Click “Write a Note” and begin typing. If your text exceeds the margin, a new window will appear with your text.

  3. Click “Save.” The Note will appear in the left margin.

  4. To delete the Note, hover your cursor over the Note. Click the drop-down icon and click “Delete.”

ADDING A WHITEBOARD

The “Whiteboard” feature allows you to visually depict concepts and then add those depictions to your class content. Whiteboards can only be created within Notes.

  1. While adding a Note, click the Whiteboard icon to add a Whiteboard.

  2. Create the Whiteboard using preferred drawing tools.

  3. Click “Save.” Whiteboards are stored within Notes in the left margin.

FILTERING INTERACTIONS

NextThought allows you to filter all Interactions, so you can view only the material that is valuable to you.

  1. Click the “Show Me” drop-down menu at the top of the page.

  2. Select any combination of filters or select “Everything” and “Everyone” to view all Interactions.

The first section in the drop-down menu allows you to choose what types of Interactions appear within your class content. The second section allows you to choose whose shared Interactions are viewable.
