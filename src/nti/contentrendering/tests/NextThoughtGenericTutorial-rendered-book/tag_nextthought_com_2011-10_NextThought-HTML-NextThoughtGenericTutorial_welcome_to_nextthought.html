Welcome to NextThought
INTRODUCTION

NextThought is an interactive social learning platform that puts you in the center of your educational experience in three key ways:

  • Instructional content at your fingertips

  • Interactive features to help you navigate content easily and annotate it how you see fit

  • Collaborative tools that offer a rich community experience

Use this tutorial to familiarize yourself with NextThought’s features.

SUPPORTED BROWSERS

NextThought works on all modern browsers except Internet Explorer. At this time there are several unique issues with the platform’s performance on each browser, and there may be times when using one browser is preferable to another. Currently we prefer the following browsers in ranked order: (1) Chrome, (2) Safari, (3) Firefox. Mobile browsers do not work at this time, but we are developing a native iPad application for the future.
