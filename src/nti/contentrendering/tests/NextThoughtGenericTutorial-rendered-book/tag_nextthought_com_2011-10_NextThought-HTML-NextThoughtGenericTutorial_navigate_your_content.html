Navigate Your Content
NAVIGATING CONTENT AND SECTIONS

Three navigation options allow you to access and move through this content.

Option 1—Library Tab

All of your class content is located in the “Library” tab at the top left-hand corner of the browser.

  1. Click on the content title in the Library tab. A new menu will slide out to the right.

  2. Click on the section you would like to view.

Option 2—Quick Jump Menu

At the top of the page, two menus indicate the current section you are viewing.

  1. Click the top menu to navigate through different topics or units.

  2. Click the bottom menu to navigate through the sections of your content.

Option 3—Navigation Arrows

Use the left and right arrows to move backward and forward, respectively, one section at a time.

SEARCHING FOR KEYWORDS AND PHRASES

The “Search” tab allows you to quickly locate keywords or phrases within content and Interactions.

  1. Click the Search tab.

  2. Type a keyword or phrase into the Search box.

  3. View your search results. Results are sorted by the types of content or Interactions in which they are located (e.g., Books, Note, Highlight).

  4. Click the drop-down arrow within the Search box to filter what types of content and Interactions are visible.

  5. Search for exact or partial matches.

HIDING THE LEFT PANEL

If you are accessing NextThought from a smaller computer screen, click the arrows in the bottom left-hand corner of your browser to hide or view the left panel allowing you to view more of the left margin.
