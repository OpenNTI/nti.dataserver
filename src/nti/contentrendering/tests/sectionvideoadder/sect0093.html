Prealgebra : Problem-Solving Strategies : Summary
15.5 Summary

In this chapter, we discussed four powerful problem-solving strategies. As you continue your math studies, you will use these strategies on all sorts of problems:

  • Find a pattern

  • Make a list

  • Draw a picture

  • Work backwards

All four of these strategies have a common goal:

Simplify the problem.
