One reason that percents are so commonly used is that they are easy to represent using decimals. As we’ve already seen, decimals are nice because they let us use our familiar base-10 number system to write fractions as well as integers.   • Convert the words to mathematics.

  • Solve the mathematical problem.

  • Write your answer in terms of the word problem, and make sure that it makes sense.
