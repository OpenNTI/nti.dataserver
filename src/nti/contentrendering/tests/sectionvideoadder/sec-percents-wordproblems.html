8.2 Word Problems

Because percents are used frequently in the real world, they show up a lot in word problems. Word problems involving percents are really no different than any other sort of word problem. The important steps to remember are:

  • Read the problem carefully!

  • Convert the words to mathematics.

  • Solve the mathematical problem.

  • Write your answer in terms of the word problem, and make sure that it makes sense.
