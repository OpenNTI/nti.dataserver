Review Problems

  1. I have four dogs whose average weight is 63 pounds. I also have three cats. The average weight of all seven of my animals is 41 pounds. What is the average weight of my cats?

  2. The average age of the ten people on my basketball team was 13.5, but then a 15-year-old joined our team and an 11-year-old quit the team. What is the average age of my team now?

  3. The average of five numbers is 18. Let the first number be increased by 1, the second number by 2, the third number by 3, the fourth number by 4, and the fifth number by 5. What is the average of the list of increased numbers? (Source: MOEMS)

  4. Suppose you took eight math tests this semester. If your average score on your first six tests was 84 and your average score on all eight tests was 86, then what was the average of your last two test scores?

  5. Must the median of a group of consecutive integers equal the average of the group?

  6. Larry writes a list of numbers that has average 14, median 21 and mode 11.

    1. Moe creates a list by adding 12 to each number in Larry’s list. What are the average, median, and mode of Moe’s list?

    2. Curly creates a list by multiplying each number in Larry’s list by 2. What are the average, median, and mode of Curly’s list?
