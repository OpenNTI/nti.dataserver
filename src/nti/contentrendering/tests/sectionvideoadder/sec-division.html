There is good news, though. Remember that we defined division in terms of multiplication (and reciprocals). And multiplication is commutative and associative. To solve division problems:

  1. Change all divisions to multiplications.

  2. Rearrange the multiplications using the commutative and associative properties.

  3. [Optional] Change some of the multiplications back to divisions.
